Welcome to Break the Silence

—About Us—


Break the Silence is a global movement dedicated to eradicating Gender-Based Violence (GBV) in all its forms. We believe that every individual has the right to live free from violence and fear, regardless of gender identity or expression. Our mission is to raise awareness, provide support services, and advocate for systemic change to end GBV once and for all.

Through education, community outreach, and empowerment programs, we strive to create a world where everyone can live with dignity and respect. Join us in our fight to break the silence and end gender-based violence for good.

—Why It Matters—


Gender-Based Violence (GBV) is a pervasive and deeply-rooted issue that affects millions of individuals worldwide on a daily basis. It encompasses various forms of violence, including physical, sexual, emotional, and economic abuse, targeting individuals based on their gender identity or expression.

At its core, GBV violates fundamental human rights and undermines the dignity, autonomy, and well-being of survivors. It perpetuates cycles of trauma, fear, and inequality, hindering individuals from fully participating in society and realizing their potential.

GBV not only inflicts immediate harm on survivors but also has far-reaching consequences for families, communities, and societies as a whole. It perpetuates harmful gender norms and inequalities, perpetuates cycles of poverty, and undermines social cohesion and economic development.

Addressing GBV is not only a moral imperative but also a strategic priority for achieving gender equality and sustainable development. By tackling the root causes of GBV, promoting gender-responsive policies, and providing comprehensive support services for survivors, we can create a world where everyone can live free from violence and fear.

Together, let us stand in solidarity with survivors, challenge harmful norms and attitudes, and work towards building a future where GBV is not only condemned but eradicated in all its forms.
